Assessment Task Detail and Instructions:

This assessment element will evaluate your practical understanding of an embedded system as well as your understanding of the relevance of the embedded computing applications impact on society, in the coming years, emphasizing the ethical and environmental implications. The requirement is for two files to be submitted, one report and one video, and that they must both be submitted at the same time. 



The assessment consists of two parts:



Part 1: Written Report based on the Coursework, accounting for 50% of the final mark

You will write a research report with the requirement:



Critically analyse the future challenges of embedded computing applications on society, emphasizing the environmental aspects



&nbsp;



The word count limit of the report is 1500 words. 



Your final document must follow an appropriate academic format, must not contain English/grammar errors and should follow appropriate referencing styles.



The type of file can be doc or pdf, submitted as a single file, or together with the part 2.



Read the marking scheme below for more information.



An example on what is expected in terms of structure, format, content and referencing style, is given here MOD007361 Report Sample.pdf .



Part 2: Practical work based on the Laboratory work, accounting for 50% of the final mark

For the practical part you will design and implement a secure access management solution. To document the design, submit a 5-minute video which shows the system functionality and a discussion of your design approach. The design approach should feature a flow diagram. Furthermore, submit a writeup where you discuss minimum of 5 limitations of your secure access management solution. minimum 2 of these limitations should be already addressed in the solution and 3 limitations should be left for future work. Each limitation should be described with no more than 150 words. 



More details can be found in: Assignment specification.docx 



Guidance Resources:

You must include references to relevant work taken from the scholar literature (e.g. journal papers), with known author, date and affiliation.



Assessment Marking Criteria / Scheme:

The marking scheme used for Part 1 (report based on the course work) is the following:



Completeness -- 40 marks -- sufficient evidence showing that you understood the requirement and you provide a complete analysis of the future challenges the embedded applications will bring to the society and environment.

Structure -- 10 marks --  standard academic report structure, with a content, introduction, a number of sections, conclusions, and bibliography; quality of presentation, format.

Originality -- 20 marks - originality of the analysis, innovative approaches, quality of your reflections for the future challenges, individual contribution.

Content -- 30 marks -- correctness of the information provided.
